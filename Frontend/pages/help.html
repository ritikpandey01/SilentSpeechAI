Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Silent Speech Translator

How accurate is the lip reading technology?
The accuracy varies based on lighting, camera quality, and individual speaking patterns. With proper setup and training, most users achieve 85-95% accuracy for common phrases. The AI continuously learns and improves with usage.
What are the system requirements?
Minimum Requirements:
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Webcam with at least 720p resolution
- Good internet connection
- Microphone (for voice output)

Recommended:
- 1080p webcam or better
- Stable lighting conditions
- Quiet environment for better focus
Can I use the app in noisy environments?
Yes! Silent Speech Translator relies solely on visual lip reading, so background noise doesn't affect translation accuracy. This makes it perfect for noisy environments like factories, public spaces, or crowded rooms.
How does the AI training work?
The AI training process involves recording yourself speaking specific phrases multiple times. The system analyzes your unique lip movements, speaking style, and facial structure to create a personalized model. This significantly improves accuracy for your specific usage patterns.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes, privacy is our top priority. All processing happens locally in your browser when possible. Video data is never stored on our servers without explicit permission. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time.
What languages are supported?
Currently, we support 12 major languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, and more. We're continuously adding new languages based on user demand and community feedback.

Troubleshooting

Solutions to common issues and problems

Camera not working or not detected
Solutions:
1. Check if another app is using the camera
2. Grant camera permissions in your browser
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Check camera connections (for external cameras)
5. Try using a different browser
6. Update your browser to the latest version
Low translation accuracy
Improvement Tips:
1. Ensure good lighting on your face
2. Position camera at eye level
3. Speak clearly and at moderate pace
4. Use the AI training feature
5. Avoid strong backlighting
6. Keep your face within the guide circle
7. Train with common phrases you use frequently
App is running slowly
Performance Optimization:
1. Close other tabs and applications
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Update your browser
5. Try lowering camera resolution in settings
6. Use a computer with better specifications for real-time processing
Voice output not working
Audio Solutions:
1. Check if your speakers/headphones are connected
2. Adjust system volume
3. Check browser audio permissions
4. Try different voice options in settings
5. Test with different browsers
6. Ensure no other applications are blocking audio
Pro Tip: Most issues can be resolved by refreshing the page and ensuring you've granted all necessary permissions (camera, microphone) to the website.
